About UniFind

About the Platform

UniFind is a centralized Lost & Found management system designed for the University of Mauritius community. It provides a structured and reliable way for students and staff to report, search, and recover lost items. By replacing scattered communication methods with a single platform, UniFind ensures that items are properly documented, easy to access, and managed efficiently.

Our Mission

Simplify the reporting of lost and found items for the entire university community.

Promote honesty and responsibility within the campus community.

Ensure items are returned securely through a verified, admin-assisted process.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Register and log in to access the platform and manage your reports and recovery requests.

2

Report an Item

Submit a Lost or Found item report with key details:

  • Item name & description
  • Location found or lost
  • Date & additional notes
3

Search & Browse

Browse all reported items, search by keyword, and filter results to find possible matches.

4

Submit a Claim

Found something that matches? Click "I found this item" on a lost post, or "Claim this item" on a found post. Provide details to verify your connection to the item.

5

Admin Verification

Once a claim is submitted, the item's status updates automatically. The admin then reviews the claim details and either approves or rejects it.

6

Resolution

If approved, the item is marked Resolved and the admin coordinates the handover. If rejected, the item returns to Pending and remains open for other claims.

Item Status Flow

Pending

The default status when an item is first reported is either lost or found. It means no claim has been submitted yet and the item is still unmatched.

Found / Claimed

Set automatically when a claim is submitted. A lost item becomes Found; a found item becomes Claimed. The admin is notified to review the claim.

Resolved

Set automatically when the admin approves a claim. The item has been successfully returned to its owner and the case is closed.

If a claim is rejected by the admin, the item's status reverts to Pending automatically keeping it visible and open for new claims from other users.

Contact & Support

For general inquiries, feedback, or assistance, reach out through the platform's contact page. The administration team will respond and provide support as needed.
