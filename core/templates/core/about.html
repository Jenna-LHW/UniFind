About the Platform

UniFind is a centralized Lost & Found management system designed for the University of Mauritius community. It provides a structured and reliable way for students and staff to report, search, and recover lost items. By replacing scattered communication methods with a single platform, UniFind ensures that items are properly documented, easy to access, and managed efficiently.

Our Mission

Simplify the reporting of lost and found items for the entire university community.

Promote honesty and responsibility within the campus community.

Ensure items are returned securely through a verified, admin-assisted process.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Register and log in to access the platform and manage your reports and recovery requests.

2

Report an Item

Submit a Lost or Found item report with key details:

  • Item name & description
  • Location found or lost
  • Date & additional notes
3

Search & Browse

Browse all reported items, search by keyword, and filter results to find possible matches.

4

Request & Recovery

Click "Claim this item" on a found item, or "I found this item" on a lost item to submit a claim with supporting details.

5

Admin Verification

All requests are reviewed by an administrator who verifies the information and determines the legitimacy of each claim.

6

Secure Item Return

Once validated, the admin coordinates the handover — ensuring privacy is maintained and ownership is verified before items are returned.

Contact & Support

For general inquiries, feedback, or assistance, reach out through the platform's contact page. The administration team will respond and provide support as needed.
